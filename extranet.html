Extranet

An extranet is a private network designed by an organization to provide third parties with controlled access to protected business information or operations.

These third parties may be suppliers, partners, customers, or other businesses. Unlike the public internet, access is restricted via login credentials to specific, trusted individuals.

Extranets also allow businesses to synchronize operations with their partners and vendors, reducing delays, mitigating the effects of disruptions, and eliminating miscommunication. Companies may also apply extranet capabilities to improve customer interactions and service delivery.
